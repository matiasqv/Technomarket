Humanity needs new solutions for the old problems

R&D enterprises are future and present. R&D startups and new groups are blossoming and they will grow even more! Technomarket optimizes the use of resources to supplies them with affordable equipment and materials while protecting the environment.

Who we are?

Technomarket is an e-commerce company based on an online platform for dealing used and new research equipment, repair parts, service and materials. This company is made by R&D people for R&D people! We know your problems. We have solutions for your company or project.

What we do?

Technomarket promotes R&D enterprises by providing economically efficient solutions while applying environmentally friendly practices. We establish symbiotic interactions between parties that favor their development, economy and environmental impact. Technomarket provides suitable solutions for its different customers. Consolidated clients need top end technologies disregarding of price, while start ups need affordable equipment.

How we do it?

Technomarket supplies the proper equipment to the needs of its customers combining best prices, highest quality for the price and environmentally friendly practices. We promote reuse and upgrading of current equipment while encourage high end users to keep the top technologies. How we do it? We talk with our customers and learn from them.

What we really, really, want...

We know that there is no other way than progress and development. We want to promote it by letting the established help the smaller in getting bigger and generate a cycle of progress based on a proper circular economic model.
